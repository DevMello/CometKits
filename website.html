What is PCB Kits

Your coding experience
starts here.

PCB Kits are innovative and affordable 3D-printable kits designed to introduce young children to the world of coding and robotics, fostering a passion for technology and creativity. With a focus on open-source learning, PCB Kits empower the next generation to build and program their own robots, sparking their curiosity and imagination.

Tech Specifications

Arduino Nano ATMega328

PCB Kits feature an Arduino Nano ATMega328 microcontroller for versatile programming and control.

Learn More

Custom PCB with L293D Motor Driver

Each PCB Kit includes a custom-made PCB with an integrated L293D motor driver and GPIO pins for an MPU6050.

Learn More

Motors: 3V to 12V, 1500-13000 RPM

PCB Kits come with motors that support a voltage range from 3V to 12V and offer a wide RPM range from 1500 to 13000, making them versatile for various applications.
